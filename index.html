Munich, Germany

Overview

Munich aka München is the capital of Bavaria, located in southern Germany. Munich has a rich history and is a hub of German culture, industry, and innovation. Founded in 1158, it has evolved into a cultural and political center. Munich is famous for its world-renowned Oktoberfest, traditional Bavarian beer and cuisine, and iconic landmarks like Marienplatz and Nymphenburg Palace. It is also the headquarters of world-renowned BMW cars.

. Attractions

    1. Marienplatz – The heart of the city, featuring the stunning New Town Hall and the famous Glockenspiel.

    2. Nymphenburg Palace – A magnificent baroque palace with beautiful gardens and lavish interiors.

    3. Deutsches Museum – The world’s largest museum of science and technology, offering fascinating exhibits for all ages.

    4. English Garden – One of the largest urban parks in Europe, perfect for leisurely walks,picnics and even surfing on the Eisbach River.

    5. Oktoberfest - One of the major festival in Germany(Beer festival)
